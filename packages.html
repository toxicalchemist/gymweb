Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice for monthly plans. Quarterly and annual plans have commitment periods.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for a small administrative fee.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students get 15% off all plans with a valid student ID.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades are immediate, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
